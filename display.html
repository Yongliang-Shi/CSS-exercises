By exploring Telco customer churn data, the project aims to identify the top 5 drivers for customer churn at Telco and construct a machine learning classification model that accurately predicts customer churn. The customer data analysis suggests that by targeting the right market, Telco’s churn rate may reduce by 6% and level with the industry average. K-Nearest Neighbors are selected as the top model. Its accuracy is 82.8% and the model beats the baseline by nearly 10%.
